Pharmaceutical Sales Force Automation

Lumo SFA Design System

A flat, clinical component library for field sales reps who log doctor visits, manage a doctor directory, and read analytics — often on a phone in the field. Structure comes from borders, spacing, and type; the one primary color is spent only on actions and status. Every section below expands to show its options.

Flat & minimal 4px radius · 4px spacing System font · 14px base Vanilla CSS custom properties
